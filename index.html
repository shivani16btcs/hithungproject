Hit or Hung

    Rules

  1. There are 2 teams: Team A with red color and Team B with blue color
  2. Both teams have 6 chances, which means they can press the "Hit" button only six times
  3. Whenever a team presses the "Hit" button, a random point is generated and added to the total score of that team
  4. After pressing the "Hit" button, the radio button of that team becomes disabled until the other team presses its "Hit" button
  5. When both teams press the "Hit" button 6 times, the result is declared to determine which team has won the game
  6. To restart the game, simply press the "RESTART" button
