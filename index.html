Hit or Hung

Rules

  1. There are two teams: Team Red (with red color) and Team Blue (with blue color).
  2. Each team has six chances to press the "Hit" button.
  3. Pressing the "Hit" button generates a random point and adds it to the team's total score.
  4. After pressing the "Hit" button, that team's radio button becomes disabled until the other team takes its turn.
  5. When both teams press the "Hit" button six times, the game results are declared to determine the winner.
  6. To restart the game, simply press the "RESTART" button.
